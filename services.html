    PAY-AS-YOU TRASH(TRASH EXPRESS WASTE DISPOSAL).

    Pay-as-You Trash(PAYT) is a Hadsh Technologies product. A modern and innovative approach to waste management and billing that offers numerous benefits for both individuals and municipalities. PAYT aims to incentivize waste reduction by charging users based on the actual amount of waste they dispose of. The core idea behind PAYT is to create a direct link between the quantity of waste produced and the cost incurred by the waste generator. This concept promotes environmental responsibility and encourages individuals and businesses to reduce ultimately leading to less waste going to landfills or incineration facilities.
